### Project Overview
- **Name:** Space Shooter Game
- **Type:** Arcade-style space shooter
- **Technologies:** React, TypeScript, Phaser 3, TensorFlow.js, MediaPipe
- **Timeline:** 4-6 weeks (core), 6-8 weeks (comprehensive)

### Requirements
- **Total:** 10 requirements
- **Acceptance Criteria:** 50+
- **Format:** EARS (Easy Approach to Requirements Syntax)

### Design
- **Components:** 8 major components
- **Data Models:** 5 core models
- **Correctness Properties:** 12 formal properties
- **Error Handling:** 5 categories

### Implementation
- **Phases:** 15 phases
- **Core Tasks:** 39 tasks
- **Optional Tasks:** 25 testing tasks
- **Estimated Effort:** 200-300 hours

### Testing
- **Unit Tests:** Comprehensive coverage
- **Property-Based Tests:** 12 properties, 100+ iterations each
- **Integration Tests:** Scene transitions, gesture, audio
- **Target Coverage:** 80%+

- **Memory Usage:** < 100MB
- **Load Time:** < 3 seconds
